Course Details

ABOUT THIS COURSE

Ability to Execute (A2E) is an online capability building program.

It is customized for us and intended to develop a shared skill set and common language to sustain our Transformation. A2E is designed for employees across every level of the organization: everyone can use A2E learnings to develop their strengths and work more efficiently and effectively.

The course can be accessed on smartphones, tablets, and computers. Each of the 17 A2E lessons takes approximately 15 minutes to complete.

What will you learn
A2E covers 17 topics and will enable you to build a wide range of skills. For example, it will show you how you can:
  • Prioritize effectively and get the most out of your time at work
  • Mitigate and avoid risks, supporting our overall culture of safety
  • Engage with presence and better connect with your colleagues
  • Have courageous conversations in which you give structured and constructive feedback
  • Drive innovation and seize opportunities for continuous improvement
